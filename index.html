OUR SERVICES

Transport

We offer efficient transport to collect your ore to the mill.

Ore Sampling

We take samples of your ore to check the capacity of gold it holds.

Grade Analysis

Gold and precious metal assays (e.g., Fire Assay, ICP-MS, AAS)

Failure Analysis

Calculating the probability of a sample's failure so as to reduce the risk of production.

Mineralogical and Metallurgical Testing

Mineral identification and quantification (e.g., XRD, SEM/EDS). Liberation and grain size analysis. Testing for amenability to gravity separation, flotation, leaching, or magnetic separation.
