ABOUT AE LABORATORIES

Who We Are

AE Laboratories is a modern metallurgical and mining support lab committed to providing professional, efficient, and affordable services to miners and industry players across Zimbabwe and the region.

What We Do

  • Gold ore sampling and analysis
  • Ore transport and logistics to milling plants
  • Short professional training courses in mining, accounting, and public relations
  • Tailored consulting for small-scale miners

Why Choose Us

We combine technical expertise with real-world experience to deliver actionable results. Our team is dedicated to quality service, safety, and innovation.
